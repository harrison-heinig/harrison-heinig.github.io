Praise Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

Overview

1022 Productions is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard data in the Praise application.

Information Collection

To better understand and improve our service, limited data may be collected. If you opt in to Apple analytics, Apple may receive anonymous analytics data. Praise also uses TelemetryDeck to collect fully anonymous usage analytics. Praise uses RevenueCat to manage purchases and subscriptions, and RevenueCat may process purchase-related analytics data required to provide those services.

In the event of an error, technical diagnostics may be collected. This may include your device IP address, device name, operating system version, app configuration details, and log data.

Data Usage

We use collected data only to improve app quality, reliability, and performance. We do not sell, rent, or purchase user data.

TelemetryDeck TelemetryClient

Praise uses TelemetryDeck for anonymous analytics reporting. See TelemetryDeck's privacy policy for more information.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect user data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, including access controls and other reasonable safeguards.

Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes are effective when posted on this page. You are encouraged to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this policy, please contact 1022 Productions through your standard support channel.
